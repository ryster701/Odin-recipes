Loaded potato dippers recipe

Steps to make loaded potato dippers

Fabulously crispy potato skins, in perfect dipping-sized pieces all you need to do is choose is what to put with them. We’re partial to them with chilli con carne as a gluten-free alternative to nachos with but wouldn’t say no to a soured cream and chive dip, either…

Ingrediants

  • Potatos
  • Cheese
  • Bacon

Steps

  1. Cut potatoes.
  2. Add shredded cheese
  3. Add Bacon
